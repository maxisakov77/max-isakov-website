Services

AI Implementation & Custom Software

I build custom technology that integrates seamlessly into architectural workflows — eliminating bottlenecks, automating error-prone tasks, and scaling operations to levels impossible through manual methods. Where others spend days on compliance calculations, my systems deliver in seconds.

Custom-Built Solutions

  • Zoning & Development Feasibility Engine – Full-stack application calculating FAR, unit counts, and development scenarios instantly. Auto-generates reports and submission documents. Integrates NYC Open Data in real-time. 50+ API endpoints.
  • Document Analysis Engine – Processes 500,000+ documents. Extracts design guidelines, creates compliance baselines, validates building applications automatically. Pattern recognition catches issues humans miss.
  • LiDAR Building Scanner & Floor Plan AI – Walk through a building, walk out with floor plans. Custom LiDAR system generates documentation from point cloud data. Same-day as-built delivery.
  • Video Analytics Platform – 50,000+ videos analyzed for movement patterns and behavioral insights. Person Re-Identification tracks individuals across camera feeds. Building usage analysis at scale.
  • Hardware & Embedded Systems – Custom 3D printers, Raspberry Pi automation, Arduino sensor arrays. Full hardware-to-software integration.

Technical Stack

  • AI & Data – AI-Directed Development, Computer Vision, Document Intelligence, LiDAR/Point Cloud, Constraint Optimization
  • Development – React, TypeScript, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, REST APIs
  • Hardware – Raspberry Pi, Arduino, Accelerometers, Sensors, 3D Printer Builds

NYC Multi-Family & Affordable Housing

Full-service architecture for NYC developers, property owners, and businesses. From zoning analysis through construction documentation, I help clients maximize their sites while creating spaces people want to inhabit.

City of Yes & UAP Expertise

I am an expert in City of Yes regulations and Universal Affordability Preference (UAP), helping clients navigate NYC's most significant zoning reform since 1961:

  • UAP Density Bonuses – Expert guidance on how Universal Affordability Preference unlocks 20% additional FAR
  • Parking Requirements – Identifying transit zones where costly parking requirements are eliminated or reduced
  • Envelope Analysis – Confirming that maximum FAR (up to 18.0 in R12 districts) is actually buildable within height and setback limits
  • Feasibility Studies – Converting zoning massing into accurate leasable area and cost estimates

Regulatory & Compliance Expertise

  • Affordable housing compliance calculations and income band requirements
  • MIH (Mandatory Inclusionary Housing) program requirements
  • 485-x tax incentive program eligibility and documentation
  • NYC Zoning Resolution interpretation and FAR optimization
  • DOB NOW filing systems and professional certification
  • PLUTO, PAD, and NYC Open Data for property research

Project Types

  • Multi-family residential (R6 through R12 districts)
  • Affordable housing under HPD programs (MIH, 485-x)
  • Mixed-use developments
  • Commercial renovations and fit-outs
  • Residential alterations (Alt-1, Alt-2, Alt-3)
  • Adaptive reuse and industrial/warehouse retrofits
