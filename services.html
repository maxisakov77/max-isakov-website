Services

From NYC residential developments to specialized institutional facilities, I bring the same commitment to every project: thoughtful design, technical precision, and spaces that serve people well.

Residential & Commercial Architecture

Full-service architecture for NYC developers, property owners, and businesses. From zoning analysis through construction documentation, I help clients maximize their sites while creating spaces people want to inhabit.

The City of Yes Advantage

I've developed proprietary tools that turn weeks-long zoning studies into rapid, data-driven feasibility assessments:

  • Instant UAP Calculations – Immediately determine how Universal Affordability Preference unlocks 20% density bonuses
  • Parking Elimination Analysis – Identify transit zones where costly parking requirements are eliminated
  • Envelope Verification – Confirm that maximum FAR (up to 18.0 in R12 districts) is actually buildable within height limits
  • NSF-to-Cost Automation – Convert zoning massing directly into accurate leasable area and cost estimates

Capabilities

  • NYC Zoning Analysis – Deep expertise in City of Yes reforms, high-density residential (R6-R12)
  • Feasibility Studies – FAR calculations, setback analysis, buildable area optimization
  • Full Project Phases – Predesign, Schematic Design, Design Development, Construction Documentation
  • DOB Coordination – Filing strategy, expediter coordination, approval navigation
  • Sustainable Design – Passive House strategies, energy code compliance

Project Types

  • Multi-family residential (R6 through R12 districts)
  • Mixed-use developments
  • Commercial renovations and fit-outs
  • Residential alterations (Alt-1, Alt-2, Alt-3)
  • Adaptive reuse and industrial/warehouse retrofits

Justice & Behavioral Health Consulting

Specialized consulting for the most demanding facility types—detention centers, behavioral health facilities, courthouses, and therapeutic treatment environments. As a recognized Subject Matter Expert, I help project teams navigate complex security requirements while creating spaces that support rehabilitation and dignity.

Capabilities

  • Security Compliance Review – ASTM F33 wall standards, physical attack ratings for barriers, doors, and glazing
  • Therapeutic Environment Planning – Layouts that balance operational security with mental health best practices
  • Custom Revit Families – Detention-specific components not available from manufacturers
  • Construction Document QA/QC – Detailed review focused on justice and security compliance
  • Stakeholder Coordination – Working with corrections officials, mental health professionals, and community groups

Collaboration with Peter C. Krasnow FAIA

For select projects, I partner with Peter Krasnow—a leading voice in justice architecture for over four decades. Together, we bring both institutional knowledge and cutting-edge technology to complex justice facilities.

Project Types

  • Detention and correctional facilities
  • Behavioral health and psychiatric hospitals
  • Therapeutic treatment centers
  • Courthouses and civic buildings
  • Intake and assessment centers

BIM & Technology Consulting

Advanced digital design services for architecture firms looking to upgrade their capabilities. I help teams implement BIM workflows, create custom content, and leverage AI tools for documentation efficiency.

Capabilities

  • Revit Modeling & Management – Project setup, standards development, model coordination
  • Custom Parametric Families – Complex components built to your specifications
  • Dynamo Scripting – Automation for repetitive tasks, data extraction, parametric design
  • AI-Integrated Documentation – Automated Room Data Sheets, area calculations, schedule generation
  • Autodesk Construction Cloud – Setup, training, and workflow optimization
  • Interoperability – Rhino, Excel, Ideate, CTC tools integration

Who This Is For

  • Small and mid-size firms without dedicated BIM staff
  • Teams pursuing large projects requiring advanced modeling
  • Firms transitioning from CAD to BIM workflows
  • Project teams needing specialized Revit content for justice or healthcare facilities
