What is City of Yes?

City of Yes for Housing Opportunity is the most significant zoning reform in NYC since 1961. It unlocks new development potential across the city through density bonuses, parking reductions, and streamlined processes—but navigating these changes requires deep expertise in both the opportunities and the constraints.

As specialists in City of Yes regulations, we help developers and property owners understand exactly what's buildable on their sites under the new rules and how to maximize their development potential.

UAP Expertise

We have deep expertise in Universal Affordability Preference (UAP), the cornerstone of City of Yes that allows developers to unlock significant additional density in exchange for affordable housing.

Key City of Yes Benefits

Parking Elimination

Many transit-rich areas now have zero parking requirements, reducing construction costs

Accessory Dwelling Units

New provisions allowing ADUs citywide on residential lots

Town Center Zoning

Faster Approvals
